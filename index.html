The legendary MrCrayfish's Vehicle Mod, fully ported and optimized for modern Minecraft versions. Drive ATVs, sports cars, and tractors with the exact classic feel you remember.

Classic Features Retained

Original Physics

The iconic, bouncy, and fun arcade-style driving mechanics have been perfectly replicated for 1.20+.

Workstation Crafting

Build your vehicles part by part using the classic workstation and jacks.

Spray Cans

Customize the color of any vehicle instantly using the legendary dye-based spray cans.

Fluid System

Fully functioning fluid dynamics. Extract sap, process fuel, and power up your engines.

Crates & Storage

Attach crates to your vehicles and transport your items across your world seamlessly.

Multiplayer Ready

Tested and optimized for modern server architectures so you can ride with friends without lag.
